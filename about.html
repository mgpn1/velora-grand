Welcome to The Velora Grand!

The Velora Grand is Goa's premier luxury beachfront hotel, offering an unparalleled experience of Indian hospitality combined with world-class amenities. Our hotel is strategically located along the pristine shores of Goa, providing guests with breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea and easy access to the region's most beautiful beaches.
